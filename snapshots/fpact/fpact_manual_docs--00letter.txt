Subject: Family PACT Bulletin and Manual Updates
Dear Family PACT Providers:
As enrolled Family Planning, Access, Care and Treatment (Family PACT) Program providers, the Medi-Cal
website (www.medi-cal.ca.gov) is your main source for access to the Family PACT Policies, Procedures
and Billing Instructions (PPBI) manual, monthly bulletin updates, manual replacement pages (MRPs) and
other program-related special mailings. The Family PACT PPBI manual is the provider’s resource for
successful incorporation of the Family PACT Program into your practice. The Family PACT PPBI and
Medi-Cal provider manuals are available online at www.medi-cal.ca.gov/go/manuals. Bulletins and MRPs
may be accessed online at www.medi-cal.ca.gov/go/bulletins. Family PACT special announcements, such
as Clinic Practice Alerts, may be accessed online at (www.familypact.org).
As an enrolled Family PACT provider, it is your responsibility to ensure that all of your practice members,
associate practitioners and ancillary staff are informed of Family PACT Program policies and procedures;
understand and follow current administrative practices; and deliver services consistent with the Family
PACT Standards.
We look forward to an effective partnership with you in the delivery of Family PACT services. If you have
Family PACT billing questions, please call the Telephone Service Center at 1-800-541-5555. If you
have Family PACT administrative or clinical questions, please call the Office of Family Planning at
(916) 650-0414, or email us at fampact@dhcs.ca.gov.
